French, the Language of Love

The language of French is universally also known as the language of love. France and its history is know for romance and love. The first thing you notice about French are the gestures. The language itself has a theatrical style of romance embedded in it. Given that French originated from Latin, it is interesting how it developed into the language that it is today. The similarities of French to English also make it a popular language to learn for English speakers world wide. There are ample words in French to express one's love and feelings and this is what makes French a most romantic language.
Paris itself is a destination for lovers. From the Eiffel Tower to the Museum D'Louvre, it is a fascinating getaway for lovers and those lost in romance. People say a trip to Paris is a trip to romance. You will find the most romantic settings and restaurants in Paris. Eat the most delicous meals while you lounge in a relaxing French atmosphere. French restaurants are packed throughout the year for romantic cuisines and couples who want to get away.
French has a soothing sound which makes it pleasant to the ear, and even the most simplest statements sound fascinating. The language is infused with a touch of class and politeness. It is also a calm and pleasing language which is possibly why many woman world wide are smitten by it. To learn more French, you may want to visit:
